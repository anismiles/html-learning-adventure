When I started this project, I knew I wanted to showcase not just my finished artwork, but the entire creative process. That's why I included time-lapse videos, audio commentary, and interactive elements.

The most important thing I learned is that multimedia isn't just about adding videos and audio. It's about creating an experience that tells a story and engages people in multiple ways.
