Latest Painting: Sunset Dreams

Just finished this watercolor piece inspired by yesterday's beautiful sunset. The way the orange and purple mixed in the sky was absolutely magical!

This piece taught me so much about color blending and patience. I spent almost 3 hours getting the gradient just right, but it was totally worth it. The key was working wet-on-wet for the sky and letting each layer dry completely before adding the trees.

I'm really excited about this piece because it combines two of my favorite things - math and art! The way colors blend follows mathematical principles, and understanding ratios helped me mix the perfect sunset colors.

üí° Art Tip: Always let each layer dry completely before adding the next! This prevents muddy colors and gives you crisp, clean edges.

What's Next?

I'm planning to create a whole series of sunset paintings, each one capturing different times of day and weather conditions. Stay tuned for more colorful adventures!
